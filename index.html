What is Science Olympiad?

Science Olympiad is a national team competition where students compete in 23 events covering all major fields of STEM. It's a fantastic way to apply classroom knowledge to real-world challenges.

Events are broken down into three categories: Study Events (like Astronomy and Anatomy & Physiology), Lab-based Events (like Forensics and Circuit Lab), and Build Events (like Flight and Scrambler). You and your partner will choose a few events to focus on, and through research, practice, and building, you'll prepare to compete against other schools.

Our Story

The Sunset Armadillos Science Olympiad team was founded in 2025 by a small group of students who had done Science Olympiad competetively in middle school and wanted to bring their passion to Sunset. We have grown into a team of 12 and are looking for more people to join us as we build our team.

Our story is built on late nights of studying, the excitement of a successful build, and the camaraderie of a team working toward a common goal. We are proud of our journey and the tradition of academic rigor and teamwork that we continue to uphold.
