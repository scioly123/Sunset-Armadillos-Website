What is Science Olympiad?

Science Olympiad is a national team competition where students compete in 23 events covering all major fields of science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM). It's a fantastic way to apply classroom knowledge to real-world challenges.

Events are broken down into three categories: **Study Events** (like Cell Biology and Anatomy & Physiology), **Lab-based Events** (like Forensics and Chemistry Lab), and **Build Events** (like Flight and Scrambler). You and your partner will choose a few events to focus on, and through research, practice, and building, you'll prepare to compete against other schools.
